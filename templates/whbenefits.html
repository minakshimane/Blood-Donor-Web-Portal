There’s no substitute for blood donation and giving blood regularly saves lives.
One single donation can be split into three separate parts, helping, saving or improvising the lives of patients. Just three teaspoons of blood can save the life of a premature baby. If you donate blood, you can help in saving lives of many people.
Accident and burns victims –
A single car accident victim can require as many as 100 units of blood.
Cancer patients –
It is estimated that more than 1 million people are diagnosed with cancer every year; most cancer patients will require blood, sometimes daily, or during their chemotherapy treatment.
